Multimeter



In this project I designed and created a multimeter capable of measuring voltage and current up to 10V and 10mA.
This project allowed for the introduction of hands-on learning in my program at Conestoga College, allowing us to
familiarize ourselves with a variety of different skills while also bringing some of the theory that we had been
learning in our classes to a practical setting.

PCB Design
The PCB was designed using Altium PCB design software, with the shape and pinout designed to interface with an
arduino UNO enabling the multimeter to be connected as a shield.

Enclosure Design
The Enclose was designed in SOLIDWORKS and later 3D Printed
