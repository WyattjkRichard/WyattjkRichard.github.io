Pokémon Mystery Gift Guide

Pokémon Mystery Gift is a feature introduced in the second generation of Pokémon games that allowed players to receive exclusive Pokémon, items, and other content through special distributions. These distributions were typically tied to real-world events and often required players to be at specific locations, such as game stores or conventions, making them difficult to access for many fans. Today, with the help of modern tools, emulators, and workarounds, it's possible to replicate or restore these events on original hardware. This guide covers the methods available for using Mystery Gift in Generations II through V.

Generation 2

Pokémon Gold, Silver, & Crystal

Method 1: Bayleef's IR ROM

Requirements:

  • Two Gameboy Colours
  • GBC Flash Cart
  • A copy of Pokémon Gold, Silver, or Crystal

Preperation:

  • Download Bayleef's IR ROM and load it onto the GBC Flash Cart
  • Progress the game until Goldenrod Department

Instructions:

  • Load the IR ROM on one GBC
  • Load the Pokémon game on the other GBC
  • Go to the Goldenrod Department Store and talk to the little girl on the fifth floor.

Generation 3

Pokémon Ruby, Sapphire, Emerald, Firered, & Leafgreen

Method 1: Goppier's Project Wonder

For the Following Games: Pokémon Emerald, FireRed, LeafGreen

Requirements:

  • Two Gameboy Advances
  • Two Gameboy Advance Wireless Adapters
  • GBA Flash Cart
  • A copy of Pokémon Emerald, FireRed, or LeafGreen

Preperation:

  • Follow Goppier's guide to prepare the distrubution rom files

Instructions:

  • Connect the Gameboy Advance Wireless Adapters to each Gameboy Advance
  • Load the distribution rom on one Gameboy Advance
  • Load the Pokémon game on the other Gameboy Advance
  • Select the Mystery gift option
  • Select Wondercard
  • Select Wireless Communication

Method 2: Goppier's Gen 3 Pokémon Distribution

For the Following Games: Pokémon Ruby, Sapphire, Emerald, FireRed, LeafGreen

Requirements:

  • Two Gameboy Advances
  • One Link Cable
  • GBA Flash Cart
  • A copy of Pokémon Ruby, Sapphire, Emerald, FireRed, or LeafGreen

Preperation:

  • Download the desired distribution ROM and load it onto the GBA Flashcart

Instructions:

  • Follow the instructions listed on github

Generation 4

Pokémon Diamond, Pearl, Platinum, Heartgold, & Soulsilver

Method 1: Poké Classic Network

Requirements:

  • One of the following wireless access points running on the 2.4 GHz band with compatible security settings (WEP or unsecured)
    • A router with WEP security and 2.4 GHz compatibility
    • A mobile phone hotspot with security turned off and 2.4 GHz compatibility
  • Any Nintendo DS system
  • A copy of Pokémon Diamond, Pearl, Platinum, Heartgold, or Soulsilver

Preperation:

  • Set the DNS to the ip listed at Poké Classic Network
  • Unlock Mystery gifts
    • In Pokémon Diamond, Pearl, and Platinum Follow the instructions listed here
    • In Pokémon HeartGold and SoulSilver, the Mystery Gift system is automatically unlocked after receiving the Pokédex

Instructions:

  • From the menu screen choose to receive a gift over Nintendo WFC
